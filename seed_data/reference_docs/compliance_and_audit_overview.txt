# Compliance & Audit Overview

## Compliance Program
AluminaTech maintains a formal compliance program aligned to customer security expectations and applicable regulations.
Controls are mapped to recognized frameworks and reviewed on a recurring basis.

## Independent Assessments
The company undergoes an annual SOC 2 Type II audit covering Security, Availability, and Confidentiality trust criteria.
Supporting evidence is collected continuously to maintain audit readiness.

## Internal Control Reviews
Quarterly internal control checks validate key controls for access governance, change management, and incident response.
Control deviations are documented with owners and remediation deadlines.

## Security Testing
Vulnerability scans are run monthly, and independent penetration tests are completed annually.
Findings are risk-ranked and tracked through remediation workflows.

## Policy Management
Security and privacy policies are reviewed at least annually and after material business or platform changes.
