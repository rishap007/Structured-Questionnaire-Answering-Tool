# Incident Response Plan

## Purpose
This plan defines AluminaTech's response process for security and availability incidents affecting customer data or service continuity.

## Severity Classification
Incidents are classified from SEV-1 to SEV-4 based on business impact, data sensitivity, and service disruption.
SEV-1 and SEV-2 incidents are treated as high severity.

## Response Timelines
For high-severity incidents (SEV-1/SEV-2), initial triage begins within 30 minutes of detection.
Containment actions are initiated as quickly as possible based on incident type.
Customer-facing updates are coordinated through the support and communications teams.

## Roles and Responsibilities
Incident Commander, Security Lead, Communications Lead, and Service Owner roles are assigned at incident start.
All actions are documented in an incident timeline for post-incident review.

## Post-Incident Activities
A root-cause analysis and corrective action plan are completed for high-severity incidents.
Lessons learned are reviewed with Engineering, Security, and Operations leadership.
