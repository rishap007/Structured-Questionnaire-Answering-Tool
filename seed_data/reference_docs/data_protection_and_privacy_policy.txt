# Data Protection & Privacy Policy

## Policy Objective
AluminaTech processes personal data to deliver alumni engagement workflows, fundraising reporting, event operations, and communication analytics for university customers.
Data processing is designed to meet contractual and regulatory obligations.

## Privacy Program Governance
A designated Privacy Lead coordinates policy updates, data mapping, and response handling with Security and Legal teams.
The privacy program follows privacy-by-design principles during product and feature development.

## Data Subject Request Handling
AluminaTech supports data subject requests under GDPR and CCPA, including access, deletion, correction, and portability where applicable.
Requests are authenticated, tracked in a case management queue, and completed within legal timeframes.
The default internal service-level target is 20 calendar days for standard requests.

## Data Retention and Minimization
Only minimum necessary personal data is collected for service delivery.
Customer-configurable retention settings are available for selected engagement records.
Operational metadata is retained according to security and compliance requirements.

## International Transfers
Where required, standard contractual clauses and appropriate safeguards are used for international data transfers.
Subprocessor risk reviews are completed before onboarding and at scheduled intervals.

## Consumer Privacy Rights
For CCPA requests, AluminaTech validates requestor identity and captures request categories in an audit log.
No selling of personal information is performed as part of core platform operations.
